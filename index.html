Our Services

Everything Your Smile Needs

From same-day emergencies to full smile transformations — professional dental care for the whole family in Wanaheda, Windhoek.

Dental Emergencies

Toothaches, broken teeth, knocked-out teeth or sudden pain — we see emergencies the same day. Call us and we will fit you in as quickly as possible.

Teeth Fillings

Composite fillings matched to your natural tooth shade. We remove decay, restore the tooth and leave it strong, smooth and looking natural.

Teeth Whitening

Professional in-chair whitening that brightens your smile safely and effectively. Results are visible immediately, with no sensitivity-causing shortcuts.

Dentures

Full and partial dentures crafted for a comfortable, natural-looking fit. We take careful measurements to make sure your dentures feel secure and look great.

Crowns & Bridges

Custom crowns protect damaged or weakened teeth. Bridges replace one or more missing teeth — restoring your bite, appearance and confidence.

Root Canal Treatments

When infection reaches the inner pulp of a tooth, root canal treatment saves it. We perform the procedure carefully to eliminate pain and preserve your natural tooth.

Dental Cleanings

Professional scale and polish that removes plaque, tartar and surface stains. Regular cleans are the foundation of good oral health and a bright smile.

Extractions

Safe, gentle tooth extractions performed with care and clear aftercare guidance. We ensure you are comfortable throughout and know exactly what to expect.

Cash & Medical Aid

We accept cash payments and are registered with major medical aid schemes. Speak to our reception team to confirm your plan is covered before your visit.
